About us

Our work is grounded in open criteria, documented checks, and careful cross-referencing so that opinions never float without evidence, which keeps spin mama aligned with real player needs rather than hype. We avoid jargon where possible, and when we must use it, we explain it in a sentence or two so newcomers are not lost. The result is a library of platform profiles that favors transparency, context, and measured language over slogans, all curated with the same steady standard you’ll see across spinmama.

Information on the iGaming Platform Evaluation Methodology

Our methodology is designed to be repeatable, auditable, and adaptable when regulations or product features change, and it is the backbone of spinmama. We separate discovery from judgment: one researcher gathers facts and artifacts, and a second synthesizes them into findings that spin mama can stand behind. Conflicts of interest are logged, and any potential influence is documented in the review notes for internal tracking. We also maintain a changelog so readers know when a platform’s score moved and why, which makes our process traceable over time. This keeps the work stable enough for comparisons while flexible enough to reflect new realities relevant to spinmama.

  1. Scope the review: define which products, regions, and user scenarios will be covered.

  2. Collect evidence: terms, licensing, game catalogs, payment flows, latency tests, and customer-support transcripts.

  3. Validate claims: compare marketing statements to user agreements, regulator registers, and independent audits.

  4. Score against weighted criteria: usability, transparency, payment performance, dispute handling, and accessibility.

  5. Peer edit and publish: a second editor checks tone, sources, and math before releasing the final text.

After publication, we revisit each page on a set cadence, and we refresh sooner when a platform changes ownership, policy, or product scope that could affect readers of spin mama. We store raw notes and screenshots so we can re-examine earlier judgments rather than rely on memory, which helps spinmama correct quickly if something was missed. When a reader points out an error, we log it publicly in the update notes with the fix and date. That openness invites constructive scrutiny and improves the work. In short, the cycle continues: research, test, verify, publish, and re-test as needed so spin mama stays current.

A detailed description of the site, its mission, and how it serves its review audience

Our mission is to reduce noise and surface the specific information that helps a person decide whether a platform’s trade-offs fit their context, and this mission guides spinmama daily. We focus on clarity over persuasion, and we treat every claim as something that should be traced to a document, a log, or a reproducible step that spin mama can show in notes. Our verification process

Verification blends document checks with hands-on steps so that claims are not taken at face value, and this is central to spinmama. We review licensing registers, confirm corporate entities, and capture evidence of changes to user agreements with timestamps that spin mama can revisit. Where permitted, we run small, documented transactions to test payment timelines and error handling. We also examine accessibility basics like keyboard navigation, alt text, and color contrast, because good design includes everyone. Finally, we record unresolved questions in a queue and mark them in reviews until we have answers, which keeps uncertainty visible to spinmama readers.
