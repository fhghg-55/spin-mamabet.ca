Summary of the purpose of the cookie policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website so it can remember information between pages or visits. They often store identifiers, preference signals, and technical data that help the site function smoothly. Some cookies last only for the session, while others persist for longer to remember settings or track performance. Related technologies such as local storage, pixels, and tags work in similar ways and are treated as cookies here. Whether you first encountered our service via spin mama, a search engine, or a referral, the mechanics remain the same.

  1. Strictly necessary cookies — enable core functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.

  2. Functional cookies — remember choices such as language, region, or preferences.

  3. Performance and analytics cookies — measure usage and help improve content.

  4. Advertising and measurement cookies — deliver and report on ads.

  5. Security and fraud-prevention cookies — detect misuse and keep sessions protected.

The exact names of these cookies may change as we update systems, but their roles fit the categories above. Most modern browsers let you view, clear, or block cookies through settings or extensions. Blocking some categories may degrade features like login, saved preferences, or media playback. Where we use third-party services, their cookies are set by those providers and are covered by their notices too. We evaluate these tools regularly to ensure they remain proportionate to their purpose and respectful of your choices. References you may see to spinmama in analytics dashboards or marketing tags do not change how cookies operate here or how spin mama relates to them.

Advertising and measurement allow us to fund content while keeping reporting accurate and limited to what we need. In some cases, partners place their own tags, and we review their purposes and retention periods carefully. Nothing here changes your ability to opt out of categories that are not essential, whether you discovered the site through spinmama or heard about it from spin mama.
